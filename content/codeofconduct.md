      <p>The Therkildsen Lab is committed to providing a safe, inclusive, welcoming, supportive, and harassment-free environment for everyone, regardless of gender, gender identity and expression, age, sexual orientation, disability, socio-economic status, physical appearance, body size, race, nationality or religious affiliation.
