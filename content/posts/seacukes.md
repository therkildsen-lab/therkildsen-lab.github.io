After multiple failed attempts at shipping frozen tissue, Jaime traveled to the Galápagos Islands to hand-carry back the precious samples of the brown sea cucumber, which we will use for a high-quality reference genome assembly. More details about the sample collection by our collaborators [here](https://www.facebook.com/188140874572413/posts/pfbid02hCNkzi9YyL2RrTFCnEUgqqi9a3ua47gQgTjCZ6H6wXsRqG5GmiJ5NwxJvnD3EA8Ql/?sfnsn=mo&mibextid=RUbZ1f).
